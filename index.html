Pioneering Surgeries

Innovations and Breakthrough Surgeries:

1. First in the World to describe and perform a novel keyhole Mini Supraorbital basal trans-isthmus approach to anterior insular lesions. Click here Click here Click here

2. Successfully removed largest brain tumor, a Tennis ball sized meningioma located in the anterior skull base, ever operated via Eyelid. Click here Click here

3. Pioneered in South India to perform DREZotomy for cancer-related pain. Click here

4. Implemented for the first time a rare and hybrid strategy to treat seven aneurysms in one patient. Click here

5. First in India and South Asia to perform an "Awake" Brain Bypass Surgery for Stroke.

6. First in India to describe the utility of intraoperative CT scan in enhancing precision and surgical safety in Neurosurgery. Click here

7. Along with colleagues in Switzerland, pioneered a novel keyhole approach to the tumors located in the lateral ventricle of the brain. Click here

8. First in the Indian subcontinent to assess serum S100 as a biomarker for diagnosis and prognostication of Intracerebral bleed. Click here

9. Along with colleagues at CMC Vellore, developed and described a simple tool "Vellore Localizer" for placing the shunt tube accurately. Click here
