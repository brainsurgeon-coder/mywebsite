Innovation

Pioneering Surgeries

A selection of breakthrough surgeries and firsts.

  1. First in the world to describe and perform a novel keyhole Mini Supraorbital basal trans-isthmus approach to anterior insular lesions. The Hindu · NDTV · Publication
  2. Successfully removed the largest brain tumor ever operated via eyelid approach — a tennis-ball-sized meningioma in the anterior skull base. Video · Publication
  3. Pioneered DREZotomy for cancer-related pain in South India. Read more
  4. Implemented a rare, hybrid strategy to treat seven aneurysms in one patient — a first. Video
  5. First in India and South Asia to perform an "awake" brain bypass surgery for stroke.
  6. First in India to describe the utility of intraoperative CT scanning in enhancing precision and surgical safety in neurosurgery. Publication
  7. With colleagues in Switzerland, pioneered a novel keyhole approach to tumors located in the lateral ventricle. Publication
  8. First in the Indian subcontinent to assess serum S100 as a biomarker for diagnosing and prognosticating intracerebral bleeds. Publication
  9. With colleagues at CMC Vellore, developed the "Vellore Localizer," a simple tool for accurate shunt-tube placement. Publication
